# GCC-First AI Consultancy Offering Strategy

## 1. Business Intent

This offering is designed as a **high-value, short-term, remote AI and cloud architecture consultancy** focused primarily on the **GCC market**, with **India as a secondary market**.

The objective is not to sell generic advisory, staffing, or long implementation projects.

The objective is to sell:

- clarity for high-stakes AI decisions
- architecture for production-grade AI systems
- problem-solving for businesses already stuck
- short, premium engagements that create immediate value

This business should be positioned as a **specialist CTO-level problem-solving practice**, not as a freelancer profile, agency, or outsourced development vendor.

---

## 2. Market Priority

### Primary Market: GCC

Primary focus should be:

- Saudi Arabia
- UAE
- Qatar
- Kuwait
- Bahrain
- Oman

### Why GCC First

The GCC market is more attractive for this consultancy because:

- businesses increasingly want AI adoption, but internal architecture depth is often limited
- decision-makers are willing to pay more for senior expertise and speed
- many firms want practical AI direction without hiring a full-time senior AI architect
- premium remote consulting is easier to justify when tied to business outcomes, cost savings, platform modernization, or execution acceleration
- short advisory engagements are easier to close than long transformation mandates

### Secondary Market: India

India should remain a secondary market for:

- selective high-fit clients
- startups with real urgency and funding
- marketplaces, SaaS, and tech-led businesses
- agency partnerships and white-label advisory

India should not be the lead pricing reference. Pricing, positioning, and tone should be anchored to GCC willingness to pay.

## 7. Engagement Model

### Delivery Mode
- fully remote
- online only
- delivered through video calls, collaborative whiteboarding, and follow-up documentation

### Engagement Formats
- 1-hour advisory session
- 3-hour focused problem-solving block
- half-day structured consulting session
- 1-day blueprint engagement
- 2-day deep-dive architecture engagement
- 3-day max intensive consulting sprint

### Duration Limits
The consultancy should remain intentionally short-term.

Maximum standard engagement: **3 days**

This supports:

- premium positioning
- focus on high-value problem solving
- reduced delivery sprawl
- faster sales cycles

### Working Style
- structured intake before session
- live deep-dive during engagement
- written outputs after engagement
- no open-ended support embedded by default

## 8. Pricing Strategy

Pricing should be anchored in **USD**, reflecting GCC-first positioning.

### Pricing Principles
- premium, not market-average
- tied to business value and seniority
- avoid low-ticket or hourly commoditization framing
- hours may exist operationally, but commercial packaging should remain outcome-driven

### Suggested Reference Pricing

#### 1-Hour Strategic Session
- **USD 300 to USD 500**

#### 3-Hour Problem-Solving Block
- **USD 900 to USD 1,500**

#### Half-Day Session
- **USD 1,200 to USD 2,000**

#### 1-Day Architecture / Blueprint Engagement
- **USD 2,000 to USD 3,500**

#### 2-Day Intensive Engagement
- **USD 4,000 to USD 6,000**

#### 3-Day Deep-Dive Sprint
- **USD 6,000 to USD 9,000**

### Secondary Pricing for India
India pricing may be selectively adjusted, but the business should not publicly anchor itself to lower India-first rates.

If India pricing is needed, it should be handled:

- case by case
- privately
- based on fit and upside

### Commercial Guardrails
- no free consulting calls beyond a light discovery screen
- no low-cost “trial” engagements
- no unpaid custom solutioning
- no hidden implementation commitment
